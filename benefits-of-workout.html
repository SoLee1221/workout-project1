Benefits of working out

Weight Loss

One of the benefits of working out is weight loss and helping maintain weight loss. Doing regular excerise can increase your matobolism (food your digest into energy). Also keeping track of how many calories you burn each day. When trying to loose weight always factor in your age, height and weight. Once you know your body weight reduce your calorie intake by 500 than your body needs to maintain your current weight. This will help you loose 1 pound (0.45kg) of body per week.

Mental Health

If your having a difficult time sleeping, excerise can help you with that. Physical activities increases your body temprature, which can have a positive effect on your mind. Another mental health benefit from excerising is Brain Boost, cardiovascular excercise new brain cells, this is called neurogenesis and also improves overall brain performance. Doing regular excercise increases your self-confidence without you even knowing it for example, your climbing some stairs without getting winded to go to work instead of using the elevator. slimmer physique for better fitting clothes, your friends may even look at you differently going through your body transformation.

Muscles and strength

Regular excercise is very important in maintaining strong muscles and bones. Physical activities like weight lifting can stimulate muscle growth as long as your taking in the necessary protein intake (protein intake example, depending on your total weight you need to take 0.8grams per kg). As we all age, we may loose muscle mass and function, which clean lead to injuries. Doing regular physical activities is very important to reduce muscle loss and maintaining strength as you age.
